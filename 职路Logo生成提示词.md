# 职路 Logo 生成提示词

## 当前采用版本

```text
Use case: logo-brand
Asset type: Chinese graduate job research website brand emblem

Create a refined and memorable square logo icon for the Chinese brand “职路”.
The concept combines:
1. the exact Chinese character “职”;
2. a minimal compass needle representing reliable direction;
3. an upward winding road representing career choice and long-term growth.

The Chinese character must remain recognizable when the logo is reduced to 46 × 46 pixels.
Use a premium flat vector style with a modern Chinese editorial and seal-design influence.
The silhouette should be strong, simple and balanced.

Color palette:
- deep forest teal #0A514D;
- warm parchment cream #E7D785;
- one restrained muted-gold accent #C6A942.

Composition:
- centered square emblem;
- rounded-square outer frame;
- generous internal padding;
- thick, crisp lines;
- no fragile decorative details.

Exact text:
“职”

Avoid:
- English letters;
- slogans;
- additional Chinese characters;
- gradients;
- shadows;
- 3D rendering;
- photorealism;
- cartoon styling;
- generic briefcase, handshake or graduation-cap clip art;
- watermark.

The result should feel trustworthy, calm, intelligent and suitable for a career research tool rather than a recruitment agency.
```

## 使用建议

- 网站左上角使用图标版本，不在图标内部加入“职路”两个完整汉字。
- “职路”名称和英文副标题继续作为图标右侧的网页文字，能保证小尺寸清晰度。
- 生成后应检查“职”字是否准确。图像模型可能生成错字，不能只看整体造型。
- 最终品牌定稿建议转换为 SVG，并由人工校正字形和几何比例。
